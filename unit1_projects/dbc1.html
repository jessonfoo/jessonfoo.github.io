Introduction

About me

Hello, my name is Jesson, I was born and raised in Southern California near Los Angeles. I graduated from UC Irvine in 2013. This is my webpage for Dev Bootcamp's Phase 0 unit 1 html section. It is the first page of my education at DBC and I expect that it will be far from my last.


Why am I here?

I am attending Dev Bootcamp because I ,obviously, want to be a developer. I have been fascinated by technology and programming. However as a result of my parent's experiences, they advised me not to pursue a career in programming. This was because they saw many programmers lose their jobs because they were no longer competent, they failed to keep up with the latest programming languages.


Looking Ahead

After graduating with a degree that I didn't really care about, I couldn't figure out what to do. Time and time again, people told me that if programming was what interested me then I should do it. After all I'm the one who has to live with the decisions that I make, not my parents.

Experience

By no means would I consider my knowledge of coding "advanced," that being said I am not without experience either. As a boy I loved looking at html, even if I didn't understand it completely. I figured out how to make my own page by early highschool. During my time at UC Irvine, I took a a course in JavaScript. I am now happy to add DBC to my list of experiences with programming.
